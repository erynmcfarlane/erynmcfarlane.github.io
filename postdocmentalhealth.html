29/04/2020 I wanted to revisit this page in light of the current COVID-19 pandemic. All I wanted to say to anyone who might find this page as a postdoc looking for help, solidarity or guidance is that you’re not alone. Postdoc-ing is already stressful, and academia is a pressure cooker. This is only worse when we add the stress of a pandemic, added caring responsibilities, and concern for our own (physical and mental) health. I don’t have any suggestions; honestly, I’m completely exhausted too. Just – if you’ve found this page – I see you. Please reach out if you need to.

19/12/2019 Recently, Dr. Murielle Ålund, Dr. Ed Ivimey-Cook and I wrote an opinion piece in the POSTDOCket. We outline some specific concerns that can disproportionately affect the mental health of post docs and make some suggestions for systemic improvements.

The most important punch line is that when postdocs are feeling overwhelmed or down, there are resources to help. You are not alone.

The whole article is here: https://www.nationalpostdoc.org/page/POSTDOCket_1712, and more resources are available at https://happypostdocs.weebly.com/ .
